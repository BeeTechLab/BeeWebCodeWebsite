About Us

Bee Tech Lab is an initiative of Baptist English High School to prepare students for a digital future. Our goal is to build strong computer fundamentals and practical knowledge.

At Bee Tech Lab, we focus on real-world skills including computer basics, modern tools, and cyber awareness, ensuring students are confident and ready for today’s digital world. By combining traditional teaching with modern methods, we aim to make learning both effective and enjoyable.



Principal’s Vision

This computer lab was established under the leadership and vision of Principal Mr. DAILEE KAPE, who strongly believes that digital education is essential for student development.

His initiative and investment reflect his commitment to provide students with better opportunities and preparing them for future challenges. Through his support, the lab has become a platform where students can explore, learn, and grow with modern technology.



Founder & Lead Instructor

Bee Tech Lab was founded by Mr. ELOW KAPE, driven by a vision to make computer education simple, practical, and accessible for every student. With a strong focus on real-world skills, he has designed structured courses that cater to learners at different levels, helping them build confidence step by step.

He also leads the lab’s digital presence—developing the website, managing social media, and creating visual content.

As an instructor, he delivers interactive lessons through 3D visual learning, live demonstrations, and hands-on practice, creating an engaging and modern learning experience for students.



Mentor & Lab Guide

The lab is guided and supervised by Mr. Dely Athisii, who mentors students and maintains a positive learning environment.

He ensures every student receives proper guidance and support, helping them stay motivated, confident, and consistent in their learning journey.
